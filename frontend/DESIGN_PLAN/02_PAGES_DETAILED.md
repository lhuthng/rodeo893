# 02_PAGES_DETAILED.md — Page-by-Page Specifications

## Phase 1: HOMEPAGE

### Purpose
First impression of the brand. Hook high-income professionals with exclusivity, craft, and membership value.

### Key Sections

#### A. Hero Section (60-70vh)
- **Image**: 1 hero product image
- **Headline**: "Rare drops. Early access. Craft, not compromise."
- **Subheading**: "Join Rodeo Inner Circle for exclusive member-only flavors."
- **CTAs**:
  - Primary: "Become a Member" (Gold button)
  - Secondary: "Shop Latest" (outline button)
- **Visual Elements**: SVG decorative borders, subtle patterns

#### B. Value Proposition (3-card section)
- **Title**: "Why Join"
- **Cards**: (icon + title + 1-2 sentences)
  1. **Early Access** — See new drops 3 days before the public.
  2. **Member-Only Flavors** — Exclusive seasonal and birthday drops.
  3. **Concierge Picks** — Personalized recommendations based on order history.

#### C. This Month's Drops (4-item teaser)
- **Title**: "This Month's Highlights"
- **Grid**: 4 product cards (2-column on tablet, 1 on mobile)
- **Card Content**: Image, name, description, member badge, pricing, CTAs

#### D. Brand Narrative (Editorial section)
- **Title**: "Crafted for Discerning Tastes"
- **Text**: 2-3 paragraphs on philosophy
- **Image**: Large process/lifestyle photography
- **CTA**: "Learn Our Story" (link to About page)

#### E. Membership Mini-CTA Section
- **Title**: "The Rodeo Inner Circle"
- **Description**: 1 paragraph on membership
- **Button**: "Start My Membership" (Primary CTA)

#### F. Footer
- **Columns**: Links, Social, Legal, Newsletter
- **Color**: Light theme

## Phase 1: MEMBERSHIP LANDING PAGE

### Purpose
Primary conversion page. Make membership irresistible.

### Key Sections

- **CTA**: "Start My Membership" (very prominent)

- Early access to all drops
- Member-only flavor releases

- "Ready to Join?"
- "Start My Membership" (Gold, very prominent)

## Phase 2: PRODUCT CATALOG

### Purpose
Browse and discover products by category with luxury layout.

### Key Sections

#### A. Category Header
- **Title**: "Cookies" (or "Coffee", "Ice Cream")
- **Hero image**: Category-specific photography
- **Description**: 1-2 sentences on craft

#### B. Product Grid
- **Layout**: 4-column (desktop) → 2-column (tablet) → 1-column (mobile)
- **Product Card**:
  - Image: 1:1 aspect ratio
  - Title: H4, 24px, Playfair
  - Description: 1 line flavor profile
  - Price: "$XX (Member) | Public: $XX"
  - Member badge: Top-right corner
  - CTAs: "Learn More" + "Shop"
  - Hover: Lift card, shadow deepens

#### C. Pagination
- Show 12-16 products per page

## Phase 2: PRODUCT DETAIL PAGE

### Purpose
Deep dive into product. Build desire, explain craft, enable purchase.

### Key Sections

#### A. Hero Section (Image left, info right)
- **Product Image**: Large, high-quality (1000x1000px+)
- **Product Info**:
  - Name: H2, 40px
  - Price: 24px
  - Member pricing (if different)
  - Member badge (if applicable)
  - Quantity selector
  - **CTA**: "Add to Cart" (Primary)

#### B. Tasting Notes Section
- **Title**: "Tasting Notes"
- **Format**: 3-4 bullet points with sensory language
  - Aroma: [Description]
  - Flavor: [Description]
  - Texture: [Description]
  - Finish: [Description]

#### C. Why It Matters
- **Title**: "The Craft Behind This"
- **Text**: 2-3 paragraphs on sourcing, process, story
- **Images**: Intersperse with process photography

#### D. Pairing Suggestions
- **Title**: "Pairs Well With"
- **Content**: 2-3 product recommendations (cross-sell)

#### E. Member Benefit Callout (if early access)
- Gold/Crimson banner with early access message

#### F. FAQ / Care Instructions
- How to store?
- Can I freeze?
- Allergens?

## Design Consistency Across Pages

- **Footer**: Identical across all pages
- **Header/Navigation**: Sticky, show membership status
- **CTAs**: Consistent button hierarchy
- **Color scheme**: Light (public) vs. Dark (member)
- **Typography**: Playfair for headers, Cormorant for body
- **Spacing**: 48-64px between major sections
- **Imagery**: High-quality photography, artisanal aesthetic
