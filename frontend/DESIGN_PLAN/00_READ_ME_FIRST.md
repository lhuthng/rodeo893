# 📋 Rodeo 893 Luxury Website Redesign — Complete Planning Package

## What You've Received

A comprehensive, phased redesign strategy for **Rodeo 893** to position the brand as a luxury membership-first business targeting high-income professionals (30-50).

---

## 🎯 Strategy in 30 Seconds

**Goal**: Increase membership signups by emphasizing exclusivity and craft over discounting  
**Approach**: Phased rollout (12 weeks) focusing on membership value prop first, then product discovery, then brand narrative, then member portal  
**Aesthetic**: Fine dining luxury with real photography + SVG accents + premium motion  
**Key Insight**: Membership = early access (3 days head start) + member-only flavors + concierge recommendations (NOT just discounts)
