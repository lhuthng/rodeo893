# 05_MEMBERSHIP_STRATEGY.md — Membership Business & Messaging

## Membership Overview

**Type**: Single-tier annual subscription ($199/year placeholder, finalise with product owner)  
**Primary Goal**: Increase membership signups  
**Value Prop**: Early access, exclusivity, community access (NOT discounts)  
**Target**: High-income professionals (30-50)

---

## Membership Benefits

### Core Benefits (All Members)

1. **3-Day Early Access**
   - Members access new drops 3 days before public
   - First pick of limited quantities
   - Messaging: "Never miss a sold-out flavor again"

2. **Member-Only Flavor Drops**
   - Exclusive seasonal releases
   - Birthday month specials
   - Limited quantities, not available to public
   - Messaging: "Products reserved for the Rodeo Inner Circle"

3. **Concierge Recommendations**
   - Personalized flavor suggestions based on order history
   - Curated quarterly "picks"
   - Messaging: "Curated flavors tailored to your taste"

---

## Membership Messaging Across Pages

### Homepage
- **Primary message**: "Early access. Member-only flavors. Exclusivity."
- **CTA**: "Become a Member" (gold, prominent)

- **Primary message**: "Join the Rodeo Inner Circle"

- **Badge messaging**: "Member-Only" or "Early Access"
- **CTA**: "Join to Buy"

### Product Pages (Members)
- **Badge messaging**: "Early Access — Available Now"

### About Page
- **Positioning**: Why membership exists
- **Testimonials**: Real members explaining why they joined

## Customer Lifecycle

### Pre-Membership
1. **Discovery**: User finds Rodeo through marketing
2. **Browsing**: User views products, sees member badges
3. **Decision**: User considers joining membership
4. **Action**: User signs up on membership page

### Post-Signup (New Member)
1. **Welcome email**: Confirms membership, explains benefits
2. **First purchase**: Member makes order (ideally exclusive drop)
3. **Early access**: Member experiences 3-day early access
4. **Concierge pick**: Member receives personalized recommendation

### Renewal (Ongoing Member)
1. **30 days before**: Reminder email
2. **7 days before**: Final reminder
3. **Auto-renewal**: Membership renews
4. **Engagement**: Continue concierge picks, exclusive drops

---

## Membership Conversion Optimization

### Landing Page Elements
1. **Clear value prop**: Headline clearly states benefit
2. **Visual testimonials**: Real member photos + quotes
3. **Transparent pricing**: No hidden fees
4. **FAQ**: Address common concerns
5. **Multiple CTAs**: "Start Membership" throughout page
6. **Trust signals**: "30-day satisfaction guarantee"

### Homepage Elements
1. **Hero CTA**: "Become a Member" (primary, not "Shop Now")
2. **Value prop cards**: Show 3 membership benefits visually
3. **Product teaser**: Highlight member-only/early-access products
4. **Testimonial**: 1 member quote (credibility)

### Product Page Elements
1. **Member badge**: Visible on member-only or early-access products
2. **Clear messaging**: "Member-only" or "Available [DATE] for members"
3. **CTA button**: "Join to Buy" (non-members) or "Add to Cart" (members)

---

## Email Strategy

### Welcome Series (Post-Signup)
- **Immediate**: Welcome email confirming membership
- **3 days**: First exclusive product announcement
- **1 week**: Concierge introduction

### Ongoing Engagement
- **Weekly/Bi-weekly**: "This week's member drops"
- **Monthly**: Concierge picks email
- **Seasonal**: Holiday/seasonal announcements
- **Transactional**: Order confirmation, shipping, delivery

### Renewal Reminders (Annual)
- **30 days before**: "Your membership renews soon"
- **7 days before**: Final reminder
- **Post-renewal**: Thank you email

## Retention Strategy

### Key Metrics
- **Renewal rate**: % of members who renew
- **Order frequency**: How often members order
- **Engagement**: Email open rates, member portal visits

### Retention Tactics
1. **Surprise drops**: Unexpected exclusive products
2. **Birthday specials**: Personalized birthday flavors
3. **Concierge service**: Regular personalized recommendations
4. **Transparency**: Be honest about product availability

## Membership Operations (Backend)

### Required Features
1. Membership registration form (email, password, payment)
2. Payment processing (annual option primary)
3. Auto-renewal automation
4. Cancellation flow (allow from profile)
5. Member badge system (product tagging)
6. Early access timing logic (DATE vs DATE+3)
7. Member portal (personalized member area, dark theme)
8. Concierge data tracking (order history for recommendations)
9. Email integration (welcome, renewals, exclusives)

### Membership Data to Track
- Total active members
- Monthly signups
- Renewal rate
- Churn rate
- Average order value (members vs. non-members)
- Member lifetime value
- Email engagement
- Popular member-exclusive products

## Legal & Terms

### Required Documents
- **Membership Terms & Conditions**: Clear explanation of what membership includes
- **Privacy Policy**: How member data is used
- **Payment Terms**: Billing, refund policy
- **Product Info**: Allergen/product details

### Key Legal Points
- Auto-renewal is clearly disclosed
- Cancellation is easy (no hoops)
- Refunds: Annual memberships typically non-refundable
- Data privacy: Clear on how recommendations work

---

## Messaging Do's & Don'ts

### DO's:
- ✅ Emphasize exclusivity and early access
- ✅ Use real member testimonials
- ✅ Be transparent about pricing
- ✅ Focus on craft and community
- ✅ Make FOMO subtle, not aggressive

### DON'Ts:
- ❌ Position membership as "discount club"
- ❌ Use fake testimonials or stock photos
- ❌ Hide fees or renewal dates
- ❌ Oversell (be honest about member-only product frequency)
- ❌ Compare membership to competitors
