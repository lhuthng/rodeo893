# 00_OVERVIEW.md — Rodeo 893 Luxury Website Redesign

## Executive Summary

**What**: Complete website redesign positioning Rodeo 893 as a luxury membership-first brand.  
**Why**: Increase membership signups (primary goal) by emphasizing exclusivity and early access over discounting.  
**Who**: High-income professionals (30-50) seeking artisanal craft and scarcity.  
**How**: Fine dining aesthetic (quiet luxury) with real photography + SVG accents + premium motion design.

---

## Core Strategy

### 1. Membership as Gatekeeper, Not Discount
- **Messaging**: "Join Rodeo Inner Circle" not "Get 20% off"
- **Value Prop**: Early access (3 days before public) + member-exclusive event products (birthdays, seasons)
- **Psychology**: Scarcity, exclusivity, community access

### 2. Visual Identity: Quiet Luxury
- **Aesthetic**: Fine dining—editorial, restrained, high contrast
- **Color Palette**:
  - Public site: light backgrounds (parchment), espresso text, gold accents
  - Member portal: dark backgrounds (espresso), gold accents, premium feel
- **Typography**: Serif-dominant (Playfair Display, Cormorant Garamond)
- **Photography**: Real, breathtaking product/process photography
- **Motion**: Premium, smooth, purposeful animations throughout

### 3. Trust Through Craft Narrative
- **Every product tells a story**: sourcing, time, skill involved
- **About page**: Founder story, philosophy, member testimonials
- **Product pages**: "Tasting notes" (not just descriptions)

---

## Business Model (Clarified)

### Single Membership Tier
- **Price**: [Decided by product owner - placeholder $199/year]
- **Benefits**:
  - Early access: Products drop for members 3 days before public
  - Event exclusives: Birthday flavor drops, seasonal specials
  - Concierge: Personalized order recommendations (via order history)
  - Order tracking: Access order status anytime (online + logged-in customers)
- **Renewal**: Annual subscription (primary model)

### Product Availability
- **Standard Products**: Available to all (public + members)
- **Early Access**: Members buy 3 days before public
- **Event Exclusives**: Member-only products on special occasions (birthdays, holidays)
- **Future**: 3D product views, video content (Phase 2)

### Member Portal (Orders, Account, Exclusive Drops)
- **Background**: Espresso (#1A1410)

- **Accents**: Gold (#D4AF37), Crimson

### Phase 1: Homepage + Membership Page (Weeks 1-3) ← START HERE

### Phase 2: Product Catalog + Detail Pages (Weeks 4-6)

- Order tracking integration

## Key Decisions Captured

1. ✅ **Single membership tier** (not multiple tiers initially)
2. ✅ **Early access > discounts** (positioning as access, not savings)
3. ✅ **Mixed media**: Real photos + SVG accents + motion (not 3D or video for MVP)
4. ✅ **Phased rollout**: Homepage → Membership → Products → Profile
5. ✅ **Light public / dark member**: Clear visual distinction
6. ✅ **Membership benefits**: Early access + event exclusives + concierge recommendations
7. ✅ **Event-triggered products**: Birthday specials, seasonal drops (future roadmap item)
